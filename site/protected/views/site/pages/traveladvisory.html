Travel Advisory

For a memorable and seamless Air Travel experience and to book Lowest Airfare Tickets on, www.cheapticket.in , given below are some helpful travel tips with documentations, Passport, VISA and other suggestions, before making the International & Domestic flight bookings:

Flight Booking - Name

  • For flight booking, ensure the name & spellings are as on Passport for International, or Original Photo ID FOR Domestic, with First, Middle & Last name (No initials Permitted).
  • Travelers must present copy of printed e-ticket to enter airport and Check-in.
  • Ensure advising your correct E-mail to receive your E-tickets.
  • Provide correct Mobile Number at Origin & Destination to receive confirmation of booking on SMS, and also any information for schedule change by the airlines.

Child & Infant Discount

  • To avail of Child under 12 years & Infant under 2 years, provide Date of birth, while booking.
  • Infant are not provided Seat.
  • Child under 5 years cannot travel as un-accompanied minor.

Passport & Visa

  • Passport must be valid for 180 days from the date of outbound travel.
  • Passport must not be mutilated and have sufficient pages for VISA endorsement.
  • Hand written passports are not valid for travel.
  • In case of Special travel permit document, check with a country of destination.
  • Ensure VISA and its validity for all Transit and destinations countries.
  • Travel & Medical Insurance required for obtaining VISA to many countries.

Vaccination

  • Check vaccination regulation & obtain vaccination for some countries before flight booking.

Pets

  • Pets such as dogs, cats and birds, may be allowed to accompany passengers on board up to maximum 5 Kilos, in a water proof carrying bag subject to airlines permission and booking directly with the airlines.
  • Check country specific requirements for Health and Rabies vaccination certificates.
  • Pets over 5 Kilos, may be allowed in the flight hold as Cargo, subject to Cargo customs clearance and documentations.

Travel insurance

  • It is recommended to obtain Travel & Medical insurance, for the duration of your travel, including for transit countries, to avoid any high cost of medical treatment in foreign country.
  • Travel insurance helps to compensate, loss of baggage, flight delays or calamity.

Frequent Flyer Number

  • For Frequent Flyer & other Airlines Privilege Cards, benefits, customers are recommended to contact the airlines directly for Credits.

Special Services

  • For Elderly & Physically disabled, wheel-chairs services has to be booked in advance by the passengers with the airlines.
  • For Special Meals request must be made, directly with the airlines.
  • Some airlines charge extra for specific seat request and you may request us for the same, subject to availability.

Quick Checklist

  • Passenger must Check-in at least 02 hours in advanced.
  • Check Departure Time with the airlines.
  • Carry your printed e-ticket, with Passport or ID.
  • Check baggage rules, to avoid payments for excess luggage.
  • Do not carry liquids, knifes, lighters, sharp objects in your hand luggage.
  • Keep photocopies of your Passport and VISA in separate luggage.
  • Re-confirm your bookings 24 hours in advance.
  • Carry Medical & Travel insurance.
  • Inform your Credit card, of your possible use of credit card abroad.
  • Check Weather at destination and carry appropriate clothing.
