E-tickets?

E-ticket is a valid, e-ticket of Airlines, good, for travel on presenting the e-print at the airline Check-in counter for all Domestic & International Travel. The E-ticket copy also required for Entry to the Airport.

What is PNR (Passenger Numeric Record)?

PNR (Passenger Numeric Record) is a unique flight booking record for each passenger & booking, created by the CRS (Computerized Reservation System), and is shown on all e-tickets, in the specified area on top of the itinerary / booking. Simultaneously, an Airline PNR is also created, which is shown on the right hand side of the flight booking details, after the arrival time.

The Airlines PNR numbers are always required by Airlines for any communication with them, or at the time of your Check-in. The CRS PNR NO. Is required, or on your making any enquiry to our ‘Help desk or Contact Us’ for any information or communication.

Airlines Ticket No.?

For any communication with the Airlines or with us Ticket No. is required. The Ticket No. consist of 3 digits as Airline Code & after a blank space with 10 digit Ticket No.

Does Fares remains same?

What is “NO-SHOW”?

There are Two types of No-Show’s for booked flight or ticket.

  • If a passenger does not Check-in at the Airline’s Airport counter before closing time of the check-in, he is a treated as No-Show. Some Airlines may allow another later flight with charges, and some Airlines may not allow to travel on the ticket.
  • Change of flight or Cancellation within 24 hours of the departure, the tickets is considered as a No-Show on some Airlines & their Rules Apply.

Refund in case of Demise of the passenger or a blood relative?

Refund may also be permitted by the Airlines for a blood relative of the deceased at the discretion of the Airlines, which can take time.
